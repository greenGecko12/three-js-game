Zombie Count

Level 1: Zombie Apocalypse

This level is very simple to play. All you have to shoot the zombies. You can shoot them to your heart's content in fact.

Don't the zombies touch you or you lose a life. You start with 3 lives and you lose if you lose all three lives. The number of lives is represented by the number of hearts above the character.

Shooting the zombies will get rid of them. However for every zombie you shoot, another one will be added to the game giving you a (practically) infinite stream of enemies.

If you ever need to read these instructions again then press the "Esc" key on your keyboard. Finally, if you want to return to the main menu then press the button below.

Move: WASD
Jump: SPACE
Look: MOUSE
Shoot: Left mouse click

Click to start!! Good luck.
