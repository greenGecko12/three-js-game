Timer:

Cross the bridge

You start at one end of a plane and have to reach the other end in as quick as time as possible.

Don't the touch the red tiles or you lose a life. You start with 3 lives and you lose if you lose all three lives. The number of lives is represented by the number of hearts above the character.

Don't let the aliens touch you either or you will lose another life. You can shoot them to get rid of them.

After completing the first level, you will automatically be navigated to the second level. If you ever need to read these instructions again then press the "Esc" key on your keyboard.

Move: WASD
Jump: SPACE
Look: MOUSE
Shoot: Left mouse click

Click to start!! Good luck.
